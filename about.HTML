How We Started

Our team and founders wanted a way to be able to keep track of all the precious recipes passed down from family. It's easy to misplace, lose, or have recipe cards damaged, so we created a way for you to add all your precious family recipes in one spot, so you can easily access them, keeping them forever. Create your own account where you can keep all of your families recipes together in one, safe place.

What we Do

Let us help you add all your recipes in one place, where you can add a photo, notes, ingredients, and step by step instructions. This way you have all of your family's special recipes in one place to pass down for generations to come.
